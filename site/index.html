Union

A chartered registry of clauses, shops, and contracts for your coding agents. One versioned source of truth for every AGENTS.md and CLAUDE.md you maintain.

Article I

Clauses

Reusable chunks of agent context, versioned together in a single store.

A clause is a named markdown snippet kept under ~/.union/clauses/, organized by path the way pass organizes secrets. Every edit is committed, so the history of your prompt engineering lives in one place instead of being rewritten across a dozen AGENTS.md files. 

Exhibit B · Authoring a clause
$ union new base/identity
$ printf 'Prefer terse answers.\n' | union new style/terse
$ union clauses
base/identity
style/terse
·✦·
Article II

Shops

Your registered projects — the places a contract is composed.

A shop is a project directory that union has been told about. Registering one (organizing, in the parlance) adds it to a central ledger so agents can discover your workspaces, and so edits to a clause can flow back into every shop that ratified it. 

Exhibit C · Organizing a shop
$ cd ~/dev/my-project
$ union organize .
$ union shops
/Users/you/dev/my-project    AGENTS.md
·✦·
Article III

The Contract

Clauses ratified into a shop, delimited by markers so human edits survive.

The contract is the shop's AGENTS.md. Ratified clauses are wrapped in HTML-comment markers; content outside the markers is yours and is never touched. When you edit a clause, every contract that ratified it is updated in place — no hand-syncing, no drift, and no surprise commits in your project repos. 

Exhibit D · After union ratify base/identity
# AGENTS.md
<!-- BEGIN union:base/identity -->
You are a careful, direct collaborator. You prefer
specific answers over hedged ones.
<!-- END union:base/identity -->

Article IV

Rites & Commands

The twelve observances a member should know.

CommandMeaning
union initCharter a new store
union newDraft a clause
union clausesList the registry
union showRead a clause aloud
union editRevise & propagate
union expelRetire a clause
union organizeRegister a shop
union shopsEnumerate shops
union disbandUn-register a shop
union ratifyAdopt a clause into a contract
union strikeRemove a clause from a contract
union contractRead the shop's current contract
